We're a team of engineers, researchers, and practitioners who believe AI systems should be rigorous, observable, and explainable — not just impressive demos. Come build with us.

Culture

What we believe

◆

Precision over velocity

We move deliberately. Getting it right matters more than getting it fast — especially when the systems we build will be in production for years.

◆

Write it down

Decisions, designs, and tradeoffs are documented. We're a globally distributed team — async, written communication is how we scale quality.

◆

Critique the work, respect the person

Code reviews are direct. Design critiques are honest. But they're always about the work, not the person. Psychological safety isn't a perk — it's a prerequisite.
