Who we are

Engineering-grade AI.
Audit-grade governance.

We partner with engineering and product leaders to build AI systems that ship — and that stand up to scrutiny months after deployment.

Our mission

Make AI delivery as rigorous as software engineering

We believe AI systems should be built — and governed — with the same discipline as the best engineering organisations in the world. That means audit trails, observable behaviour, and the ability to explain decisions to anyone in the organisation who needs to understand them.

Founded in 2021, Rockmount AI has grown from a specialist governance consultancy into a full-stack AI delivery partner for enterprise engineering teams across six countries.

Book a Consultation

Systems-first thinking

We architect before we build. Every engagement starts with understanding the full system — data flows, failure modes, and downstream dependencies.

Audit by default

Governance is not a post-launch checkmark. We embed audit trails, explainability layers, and policy controls into the first build, not the fifth revision.

Human-in-the-loop by design

Automation doesn't mean removing human judgement. We build escalation paths, review hooks, and override controls from day one.

Delivery discipline

We commit to SLAs we can hold. Scope, timeline, and acceptance criteria are agreed in writing — and we are accountable to them.

Open standards preference

We prefer open models, open evaluation frameworks, and interoperable architectures — so your team retains ownership and optionality.

Our history

How we got here

  1. 21
    2021

    Founded in London

    Rockmount AI launched as a specialist AI governance advisory firm, helping financial services organisations audit and document existing ML models.

  2. 22
    2022

    Expanded to delivery

    Client demand led us to build AI automation systems alongside our audit practice — combining delivery speed with governance rigour.

  3. 23
    2023

    Series A · $28M raised

    Raised Series A to build our monitoring & governance platform, expand to Singapore and Berlin, and grow the audit team to 60 specialists.

  4. 25
    2025

    120+ AI systems audited

    Now operating in 6 countries with 34 enterprise clients and a team of 180 engineers, researchers, and governance specialists.
